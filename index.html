Privacy Policy

This Chrome Extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data of users.

The extension communicates with https://www.altered.gg to access user-related game data for display purposes only. No information is stored, tracked, or transmitted elsewhere.

We do not use analytics, cookies, or any form of third-party tracking.

All data stays within the browser session and is only used to enhance the functionality of the Altered platform.

If you have any questions, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
